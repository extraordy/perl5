Formatted Printing and More Looping - Quiz 2

Is a fixed-length field appropriate for printing the following data types? Justify your answer:

  • A person's last name
  • A telephone number
  • An airline flight number

There is not a single correct answer; one should suggest that a flight number and/or telephone number might make a good candidate, but the focus here is for them to justify their answer.

Grading Criteria: Student should show understanding of fixed length fields.
