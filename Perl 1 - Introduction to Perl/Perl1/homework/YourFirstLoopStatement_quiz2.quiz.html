Your First Loop Statement - Quiz 2

Without running the following code, provide the values of $x and $y after the code runs:

Observe:
$x = 4; 
$y = $x++ - 3;

$x == 5 && $y == 1 due to the postfix increment not taking effect until after the statement.

Grading Criteria: Student should correctly describe the delayed effect of a postfix operator.
