Scalars and Arithmetic - Quiz 1

Which of the following are valid (legal) choices of names for scalars that you can declare with my?

  1. $don't-smoke
  2. $q
  3. $MAX_ITERATIONS
  4. $42
  5. $_
  6. $temp2
  7. $circle_area
  8. $xj_43__
  9. $Standard_Pressure

Which of the legal ones look like good choices of names? Give reasons for disliking the others.

Valid:
 2: $q
 3: $MAX_ITERATIONS
 6: $temp2
 7: $circle_area
 8: $xj_43__
 9: $Standard_Pressure

Invalid:
 1: $don't-smoke # contains invalid characters (hyphen and apostrophe)
 4: $42          # starts with numbers
 5: $_          # starts with underscore, advanced users will notice that this is a Perl built-in; well get to that in a later lesson

Good Choices:
 3: $MAX_ITERATIONS    # Good example of a global constant (by convention all caps)
 7: $circle_area       # Good example of a local variable (by convention all lower)
 9: $Standard_Pressure # Good example clear, descriptive name, use of _ is readable

Grading Criteria: Student should demonstrate knowledge of restrictions and standards for variable names.
