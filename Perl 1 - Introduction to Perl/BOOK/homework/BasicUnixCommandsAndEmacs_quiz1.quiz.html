Basic Unix Commands and Emacs - Quiz 1

Suppose you wanted to save your file in emacs, but you pressed C-x C-d instead of C-x C-s. How would you stop and correct it?

Press C-g.

Grading Criteria: Student should demonstrate knowledge of emacs commands.

How would you cut and paste a line of text in emacs?

C-k to cut, C-y to paste. You can also mark the beginning of the text you wish to cut using Ctrl+space and mark the end with Ctrl+w.

Grading Criteria: Student should demonstrate knowledge of emacs commands for cut and paste.
