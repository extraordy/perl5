What is the difference between /[A-Z]+\d+/ and /[A-Z]+ \d+/?

What is the difference between /[A-Z]+ \d+/ and /[A-Z]+\s+\d+/?

The space matches literally, so the second regex requires exactly one space whereas the first one requires none. The third regex requires at least one whitespace character of any kind, but more are permissible.
