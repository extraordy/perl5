Opening Filehandles - Quiz 1

What is the difference between:

Observe:
open my $fh, '<', $file || die $!

and:

Observe:
open my $fh, '<', $file or die $!

?

The || operator binds tightly so that it tests the truth of $file instead of open.

Grading Criteria: Student should demonstrate knowledge of the presence of the or operators in Perl.

How could you keep the || operator but still have the same effect as the second statement?

Use parentheses: open( my $fh, '<', $file ) || die $!;.

Grading Criteria: Student should demonstrate knowledge of how to properly manage the precedence of an operator by way of parentheses.
